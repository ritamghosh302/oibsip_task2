Why We Admire Arijit Singh

Arijit Singh, born on April 25, 1987, in Jiaganj, West Bengal, is a musical maestro whose voice resonates with millions. From the soul-stirring "Tum Hi Ho" to the vibrant "Kesariya," his versatility has redefined Bollywood music. Arijit’s ability to infuse raw emotion into every note makes him a global icon.

Beyond his vocal prowess, Arijit’s humility shines through. Despite accolades like two National Film Awards and seven Filmfare Awards, he remains grounded. His initiative, “Heshel,” a restaurant offering affordable meals in his hometown, reflects his commitment to social good. Arijit’s journey from a small town to stardom inspires us to pursue passion with dedication.

"Music is my way of connecting with people’s hearts, no matter where they are." — Arijit Singh

Key Milestones

2013

Rose to fame with "Tum Hi Ho" from Aashiqui 2, earning widespread acclaim.

2016

Won his first National Film Award for "Sooraj Dooba Hain" from Roy.

2022

Delivered the global hit "Kesariya" from Brahmāstra, showcasing his versatility.

2025

Honored with the Padma Shri, India’s fourth-highest civilian award.
